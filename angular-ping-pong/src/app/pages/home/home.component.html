🏓 Ping Pong Challenge

How to Play

  • 🎮 Control your paddle with W/S or ↑/↓ keys.
  • 🏓 Ball auto-serves after each point.
  • ⏸ Pause/Resume with Space key or Pause button.
  • 🔄 Restart anytime with the Restart button.
  • 🎯 First player to 10 points wins the match!
  • 👥 Play solo vs AI or challenge a friend online.
